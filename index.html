The Augusta RAGU (Research and Generally Useful) Lab is dedicated to developing electronics projects that involve saving the environment. Projects include low cost turbidity and pH senors, pipe crawlers, autonomous water craft, smart trash cans, parking sensors, and various other sensors. The group works as part of a collaboration with Augusta Utilities and Augusta University. Augusta Utilities sponsors the research projects. Augusta Utilities is interested in water quaility sensors, sensor monitoring platforms, and most anything pertaining to the physics, chemistry, and biology of water systems.
